What Would You Put in the Field Guide?

Writing a field guide for new instructional designers means making sure it reflects real voices from the field. While this survey isn’t part of my formal research, it’s a space for L&D pros and educators to share what they wish they had known starting out, and what tools or advice have actually helped them grow.
