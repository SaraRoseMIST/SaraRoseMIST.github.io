Instructional Designers’ Field Guide: Mini Toolkit

A free, fun planning starter set for early career-instructional designers

This downloadable toolkit includes editable templates and guided worksheets to help you plan, organize, and reflect during your instructional design process. Whether you're mapping a course, brainstorming strategies, or sketching out your storyboard, this kit is meant to make the early stages more structured — and a little more fun. You can print and complete them by hand or use the fillable PDF versions. Instructions are included on each page.
