How to Use The Field Guide

The Instructional Designers’ Field Guide is not meant to be read linearly or memorized like a textbook. It’s a flexible, evolving toolkit — designed to support real-world design challenges as they arise. Whether you're planning a course, revising training materials, or exploring new tools, this guide is made to meet you where you are.
